Why This Exists

The Internet Teaches Settings.
Experience Teaches Seeing.

There has never been more photography education available — and never more confusion. Tutorials teach what buttons do. They cannot teach how to read a face, how to shape light on it, how to survive four decades of technological change, or how to behave on a professional assignment when things go wrong.

Sudeep Kapoor's mentorship exists to pass on the part of photography that does not fit in a tutorial: judgement, discipline, and the working habits of a career that began in the darkroom and never stopped adapting.
