How It Works

The Process

Every assignment is different, but the shape of a project is consistent — and it always begins with a conversation.

  1. Consultation

    You describe the project — purpose, audience, locations, timelines. Sudeep advises on approach.

  2. Proposal

    Scope, deliverables, schedule and commercial terms, in writing.

  3. The Shoot

    In studio or on site — planned, safe, and efficient with your people's time.

  4. Selection & Delivery

    Edited selects delivered to agreed formats — print, publication or digital use.
