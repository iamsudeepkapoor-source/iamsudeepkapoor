Preservation

Why the Archive Matters

Negatives fade. Transparencies degrade. Hard drives fail. A four-decade body of work survives only if it is deliberately preserved — scanned, catalogued, dated and described while its author can still tell the story behind every frame.

This archive is that project: a living record of Indian faces, industry and photographic craft from the 1980s to today, being documented while the photographer is still actively creating.
