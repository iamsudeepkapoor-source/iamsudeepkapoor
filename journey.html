300 Feet Below the Earth

On a mining assignment, Sudeep descends roughly 300 feet underground into a working coal mine — photographing miners and machines in an environment where light itself must be carried in, under full industrial safety protocols.

Before Drones, He Climbed

To capture elevated views of working factories, he climbs approximately 300 steps up industrial towers in safety gear, camera on shoulder — achieving aerial perspectives years before drone photography made them commonplace.
